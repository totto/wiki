    CV (English)¶

    Thor Henning Hetland -- Oslo, Norway totto@totto.org | LinkedIn | GitHub

    Software architect, community builder, and AI development pioneer with 30+ years across enterprise, open source, and product. Co-founder of JavaZone, former president of javaBin, and Java Champion. Pioneer of Skill-Driven Development (SDD) -- a methodology that has delivered production systems at 25--66x the speed of conventional approaches. I build things that matter and share what I learn.

    Java Champion

    Recognised by Sun Microsystems (now Oracle) as a Java Champion -- awarded to individuals who have made exceptional contributions to the Java community through innovation, education, or open source leadership.
