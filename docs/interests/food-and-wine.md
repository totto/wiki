# Food and Wine in Norway

!!! note "Historical note"
    These lists were compiled around 2007--2009. Norwegian dining has evolved considerably since, but these remain genuinely memorable experiences.

## Best food experiences

1. **Statholdergaarden** (2009)
2. **Bagatelle** (2007)
3. **Flying Culinary Circus** (2008)
4. **Le Canard** (2007)
5. **Rica Nidelven** (2009)
6. **Engo Gard** (2008)

## Most memorable wines

1. **Chateau Haut-Brion 1998** (tasted 2007)
2. **Alma Viva 1998** (tasted 2007)
3. **Wolf Blass Platinum** (tasted 2009)
