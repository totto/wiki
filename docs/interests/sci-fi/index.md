# Sci-Fi Reading

<p class="scifi-subtitle">I read a lot of science fiction -- mostly space opera, hard sci-fi, and the occasional military SF binge. My Kindle is never empty and my to-read list never gets shorter. These are the books I have tracked from 2011 onwards, pulled from my Kindle order history. Some are great, some are forgettable, and a few completely changed how I think about technology and the future. The lists are unfiltered -- every book I finished that year, in roughly the order I read them.</p>

## Standouts

A few that stuck with me across the years:

<div class="standouts-grid" markdown>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
<span class="standout-title">Hyperion & Fall of Hyperion</span>
<span class="standout-author">Dan Simmons</span>
<span class="standout-note">Still the benchmark for ambitious science fiction</span>
</div>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
<span class="standout-title">Blindsight</span>
<span class="standout-author">Peter Watts</span>
<span class="standout-note">The most unsettling take on consciousness I have read</span>
</div>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
<span class="standout-title">Children of Time</span>
<span class="standout-author">Adrian Tchaikovsky</span>
<span class="standout-note">Evolution and alien minds done right</span>
</div>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
<span class="standout-title">The Three-Body Problem</span>
<span class="standout-author">Cixin Liu</span>
<span class="standout-note">Before it was everywhere</span>
</div>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
<span class="standout-title">Seveneves</span>
<span class="standout-author">Neal Stephenson</span>
<span class="standout-note">Brutal, brilliant, unforgettable</span>
</div>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
<span class="standout-title">Dark Matter</span>
<span class="standout-author">Blake Crouch</span>
<span class="standout-note">Perfect pacing, genuinely surprising</span>
</div>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
<span class="standout-title">Shards of Earth</span>
<span class="standout-author">Adrian Tchaikovsky</span>
<span class="standout-note">The best space opera of the 2020s so far</span>
</div>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
<span class="standout-title">Wool</span>
<span class="standout-author">Hugh Howey</span>
<span class="standout-note">Indie sci-fi at its best</span>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>

<div class="standout-card" markdown>
