# Agile Wine 2011: An Agile Tragedy

**Date:** 2011
**Slides:** [slideshare.net/totto](https://www.slideshare.net/slideshow/agile-wineaccn2011/35474438)

A critical examination of various agile practitioners and their approaches to a simple task — buying wine for a dinner date. Highlights the strengths and weaknesses of different agile methodologies through satire, and makes the case for a more holistic understanding of agile practices rather than dogmatic application of any single method.
