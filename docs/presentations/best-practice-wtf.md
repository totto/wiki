# Best Practice — WTF!

**Date:** September 2023
**Conference:** JavaZone 2023, Oslo
**Format:** Lightning talk (10 minutes) · Norwegian

The consultancy business and similar entities frequently use the term "best practice" — but let us sit back and analyse the major flaws in the idea and the concept. Because it is utterly meaningless.

Pointless use of the term often blocks innovation and smart technical choices. Intended for developers and product managers who encounter daily obstacles when attempting to create quality software.
