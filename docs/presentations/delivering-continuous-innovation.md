# Delivering Continuous Innovation: Thousands of Releases a Year with Zero Downtime

**Date:** ~2023 (second edition)

How a small team delivers thousands of releases per year with zero downtime. Covers the foundational principles, context and scope of customer work, unique approaches that set eXOReaction apart, and the controversial details of a highly optimised development culture — strong dedication, hypothesis-driven development, distributed teams, NoOps tooling, and embracing failure as the best kind of learning.
