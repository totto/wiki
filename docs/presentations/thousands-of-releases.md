# Thousands of Releases per Year, 24/7 with No Downtime, with a Team of 5

**Date:** ~2022
**Conference:** Rebel Share

*Let's talk about productivity in software development.*

The original version of this talk. Shares experiences on how and why eXOReaction does things quite differently from others — optimising for productivity and value creation. Covers effective distributed teams across time zones, ownership of solutions, technology and processes, how business-critical microservice-oriented solutions are grown for customers, and why investing in a better platform, libraries, and tooling for NoOps organisations pays off.
